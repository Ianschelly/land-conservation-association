Jake Munger has been working in the Gibbs lab within the Center for Sustainability and the Global Environment (SAGE) and the Nelson Institute for Environmental Studies since 2011. Jake provides data analysis, data engineering, and research support for Gibbs lab projects in Brazil, with a particular expertise in working with spatial data. During his time with the Gibbs lab, Jake has been a co-author on numerous papers while also contributing to several NGO projects. Prior to joining the Gibbs lab, Jake earned a BA from Brown University and a Master's from Yale School of the Environment.
